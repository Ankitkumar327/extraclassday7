Explain the difference between Border-Box and Content-Box

Border-Box and Content-Box are the properties of Box-sizing. In Border-Box, it takes only the assigned height and width to them whereas in case of Content-box, box includes height, width along with height and width it also takes margin, padding , and border also to them and as a result box size increases as compared to border-box
